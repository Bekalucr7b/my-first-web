what is ai

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence .

in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and make decisions. AI systems

can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence,such as problem-solving,

pattern recognition, decision-making, language understanding, and more

Types of AI:

1 Narrow AI (Weak AI):

- Designed to perform a specific task.

- Examples: Virtual assistants (like Siri or Alexa), recommendation systems, and facial recognition.

2 General AI (Strong AI):

- A hypothetical AI capable of understanding, learning, and performing any intellectual task a human can.
